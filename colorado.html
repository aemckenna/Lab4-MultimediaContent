Itinerary

With only 12 hours on that trip (and lots of photo stops), I made a specific itinerary to maximize our time:

  1. Drive to Boulder
  2. Stop for breakfast
  3. Drive to Estes Park
  4. Get pictures at Mary's Lake
  5. Drive to Bierstadt Lake
  6. Short hike
  7. Drive back to the airport

I love visiting Colorado, and after graduating I'm hoping to mp4e either there or to North Carolina.
