Salkantay to Machu Picchu

Last year I went to Peru for three weeks and got to see some pretty amazing things! One highlight was the Salkantay Trek and hiking all the way to Machu Picchu!

A few of the cities I visited in Peru are:

  • Cusco
  • Lima
  • Huacachina
  • Paracas

Cusco

Cusco is a bustling city in Peru and there was a lot of military activity and bands playing. I found a recording similar to what it sounded like!

Final Thoughts

Peru is such a beautiful place and it was such a fun adventure!
